---
name: nex:notify
description: View and manage Nex notifications, preferences, and custom notification rules
---

Use the Nex MCP tools to manage notifications based on the user's request.

For viewing notifications: use `nex_get_notifications` to list recent notifications.
For preferences: use `nex_get_notification_preferences` to view or `nex_set_notification_preferences` to update (frequency, enabled types, digest toggle).
For custom rules: use `nex_create_notification_rule` to create, `nex_list_notification_rules` to list, or `nex_delete_notification_rule` to remove.

Present notifications clearly, highlighting type, importance, and content.
